# THE-SUPER-PROMPTAINER-2000
A Small Tool Helps You Find The Right Words For Your Text-To-Image Prompts

As reference I have used the "Dall-E 2 prompt book" (to find on https://dallery.gallery/).

It is just a tool that chains words together. The words are those that can have a strong influence on the result of an image generation.

They are divided into several useful categories.

You don't have to use the tool only to generate and copy words.
Just taking a quick look at the categories and the words can help you find inspiration or consider an aspect you didn't have in mind before.
